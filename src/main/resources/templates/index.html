🏷️ About the System

Revolutionizing Educational Assessments

The **Online Exam Portal** is an all-in-one assessment engine built specifically to help schools, universities, and corporate institutions run paperless examinations. Designed with both user experience and reliability at the core.

Students can review available courses, register directly in just a few taps, undergo real-time exam execution with built-in timers, and secure absolute analytics reports instantly upon completion. On the other side, administrators enjoy total authority over scheduling, adding complex MCQs, updating details, and reviewing statistics.
